How long does each therapy session last? Each session lasts for 1 hour.
02
How often should I schedule my sessions? Initially, weekly sessions help build momentum. Over time, you may transition to biweekly or monthly, depending on your progress and goals.
03
How long will I need to be in therapy? Therapy duration varies. Some people see progress in a few months, while others prefer ongoing support for a year or more.
04
What if I don’t feel comfortable with my therapist? Feeling comfortable in therapy is essential. If something doesn’t feel right, you can talk to your therapist or explore other options.
05
Can I switch or stop therapy at any time? Yes, you can adjust, pause, or stop therapy whenever you feel ready. Your therapist can also help you transition when the time comes.
06
Is there a fixed sequence to therapy? No, therapy is personalized. Your therapist adapts the approach based on your needs, using techniques that best support your growth.
